Bell Hopper

Welcome to Bell Hopper! You play as the bell hop in a hotel filled with monsters. You have to tap a floor to make a bell hopper on that floor jump in this game, but make sure you don't jump onto a monster! Timing is key. Control either 3 or 5 bell hops. There is a leaderboard for both the 3 and 5 floored games. This game is compatible with Android phones and tablets. Download Bell Hopper today!

Pirates vs. Ninjas

Play as the ninja in this game! Throw ninja stars at the pirates and see how long you can stay alive. It is tough to win points in this tricky game. After playing, go to the upgrade shop and use coins to buy upgrades to shoot faster and have more lives. Music by Alex Smith. Optimized for iPads. Download Ninjas vs Pirates today!
